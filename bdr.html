New Investment Policy (since December 2017)

Building Dreams FX (BDR) is composed only by reversing strategies opening trades in the opposite direction on volatility breakouts. It is a useful complement to New Trading FX portfolio (NTR).
The idea behind this portfolio is very simple: when breakouts are activated, our algorithms analyse the strength of this movement looking for a trend exhaustion and a possible entry point for a reversal movement.
This type of portfolio is very interesting in terms of diversification. As a rule there are no liquidity problems or wide spreads because the signals are activated with trends already established.
Like NTR, this portfolio may suffer on sideways trends if there is not enough market volatility. It will also try to get stable performance maintaining drawdown below 20%.
