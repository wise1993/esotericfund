Esoteric Fund is a global macro investment fund specializing in G10 FX.

The fund seeks to compound capital across market cycles while maintaining a high margin of safety to mitigate risk.

The Fund utilizes an active, discretionary process rooted in fundamental research, proprietary analytical methodologies and quantitative analysis to express global thematic ideas that has consistently produced strong risk adjusted returns.

The investment approach is designed to benefit from volatility in currency markets, preserve and grow our investor’s capital, providing financial security while enabling a robust build-up of capital
